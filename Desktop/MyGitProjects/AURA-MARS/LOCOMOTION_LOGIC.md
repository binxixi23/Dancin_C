# Leg-Wheel Hybrid Locomotion Logic

### 1. Mode: Rolling (Cruising)
- **Active on:** Slopes < 10°, flat debris-free zones.
- **Benefit:** Low energy consumption, speeds up to 15 km/h.

### 2. Mode: Walking (Climbing)
- **Active on:** Stairs, craters, rubble.
- **Benefit:** High clearance, 4-point stability on uneven ground.

### 3. Mode: Crouching (Tactical)
- **Action:** Retracts legs to lower profile (< 50cm).
- **Benefit:** Moving through narrow gaps or maintaining a low visibility profile.
